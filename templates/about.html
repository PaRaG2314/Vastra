About Vastra

Vastra is a modern fashion e-commerce platform designed to provide a seamless online shopping experience. Our goal is to bring high-quality fashion products together with a clean, fast and user-friendly shopping experience.

🛍️ Our Story

Vastra was developed as a full-stack Django project to demonstrate the core features of a real-world fashion e-commerce platform including authentication, shopping cart, checkout, order management and user profiles.

🎯 Our Mission

To make online fashion shopping simple, secure and accessible while providing a smooth and enjoyable customer experience.

👁️ Our Vision

To build a scalable e-commerce platform that combines modern web technologies with an intuitive shopping experience for fashion enthusiasts.

💻 Technologies Used

  • Python
  • Django
  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • JavaScript
  • SQLite Database
